Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Roman pasta dish that’s creamy, rich, and irresistibly comforting — made the traditional Italian way with eggs, cheese, and crispy pancetta.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale (or smoked beef/turkey bacon)
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup (50g) Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese
  • 1 garlic clove (optional)
  • Salt (for pasta water)
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta: Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Save ½ cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Cook the pancetta: In a pan, fry pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat once golden.
  3. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, whisk eggs, cheese, and black pepper until smooth.
  4. Combine everything: Add pasta to the pancetta pan, then mix in egg mixture off the heat. Add pasta water gradually for a creamy texture.
  5. Serve: Garnish with extra cheese and pepper. Enjoy warm and silky!
